Version: 0.3
Date: 2026-07-07
Notes: - Rotation fix: writing is no longer lost when the iPad rotates
         (portrait <-> landscape). Strokes are kept and rescaled to the new
         canvas size instead of being cleared; also listens to orientationchange.
       - Pinyin hint: the reveal (eye button) and the review cards now show
         the 汉语拼音 with tone marks, generated offline via bundled pinyin-pro.
       - Thicker ink: handwriting line width increased (2.4 + pressure*4).
